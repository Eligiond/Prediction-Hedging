# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Individuals whose income, investments, or operating risks are exposed to political, economic, climate, and industry events. They use Riskoff to explain an exposure in plain language, discover relevant prediction-market hedges, paper trade them, and monitor how those hedges perform.

## Product Purpose

Riskoff turns personal context and live prediction-market information into understandable, testable hedge ideas. Success means a user can move from an uncertain real-world risk to a sourced paper-trade plan, understand why each position helps, and keep their private context under their control.

## Brand Personality

Calm, rigorous, and protective. Riskoff should feel like a capable risk analyst that explains its reasoning clearly without pretending that uncertain outcomes are guaranteed.

## Anti-references

Do not resemble a swipe-based consumer game, a speculative casino, a crypto trading terminal, or a generic AI chat wrapper. Avoid hype, urgency theater, opaque scores, and interfaces that blur paper trading with real-money execution.

## Design Principles

- Make the next safe action obvious.
- Explain the causal link between an exposure and every proposed hedge.
- Distinguish local, private functionality from public or cloud-connected functionality.
- Show sources, uncertainty, and paper-trading boundaries at the point of decision.
- Prefer familiar financial-tool patterns over novel interaction for its own sake.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Never encode gains, losses, connection state, or risk using color alone. Support keyboard navigation, visible focus, reduced motion, readable contrast, and plain-language explanations for financial concepts.
